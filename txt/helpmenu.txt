Help Menu:
----------
Player Character mode:                  

a, ARROW_LEFT  - Move left
s, ARROW_UP    - Move up
d, ARROW_DOWN  - Move down
f, ARROW_RIGHT - Move right
,              - Pick up item at feet
c              - Turn camera mode on
l              - Turn logger mode on
q              - Quit
?              - This help menu

Camera mode:

ARROW_LEFT  - Move left
ARROW_UP    - Move up
ARROW_DOWN  - Move down
ARROW_RIGHT - Move right
c              - Turn camera mode off

Logger mode:

ARROW_UP    - Move up
ARROW_DOWN  - Move down
l           - Turn logger mode off

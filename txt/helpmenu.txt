Help Menu:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Player Character mode:                  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
4                - Move west      | 8                 - Move north
2                - Move south     | 6                 - Move east
7                - Move northwest | 9                 - Move northeast
1                - Move southwest | 3                 - Move southeast
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
, - Pick up item at feet          | i - Display inventory
c - Turn camera mode on           | l - Turn logger mode on
q - Quit                          | ? - This help menu
o - Open door (then a direction)  | 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Camera mode:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
ARROW_LEFT  - Move left                 ARROW_UP    - Move up
ARROW_DOWN  - Move down                 ARROW_RIGHT - Move right
c           - Turn camera mode off
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Logger mode:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
ARROW_UP    - Move up                   ARROW_DOWN  - Move down
l           - Turn logger mode off
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Press any key to continue
